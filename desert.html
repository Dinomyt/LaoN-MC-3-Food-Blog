Coconut Pudding


Ingredient List

------------------------------------------------

  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • â…“ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon coconut extract

------------------------------------------------

Directions

  1. Combine coconut milk, sugar, cornstarch, butter, egg yolks, and coconut extract in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Cook, whisking continuously, until thickened, about 4 to 7 minutes.
  2. Pour pudding into individual portions and chill before serving, at least 15 to 20 minutes.
