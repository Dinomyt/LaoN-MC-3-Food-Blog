Beef Larb


Ingredient List

  • Sirloin steaks, amount to taste
  • Namtok Seasoning Mix
  • Fish Sauce
  • Lime
  • Shallots
  • Toasted Rice Powder
  • Fresh Thai Chili

Directions

  1. Chop some Shallots and Fresh Thai Chili and set to side.
  2. Cook Sirlion steak to medium rare or to preference.
  3. Slice steak into thin pieces.
  4. Place into a bowl then toss in your Namtok Seasoning Mix until desire taste is achieved.
  5. Add Fish Sauce and Lime until desire taste is achieved.
  6. Add Toasted Rice Powder for added texture.
  7. Toss all ingredients together and serve.
