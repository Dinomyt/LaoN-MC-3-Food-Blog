Tom Yum Goong


Ingredient List

------------------------------------------------

  • Shrimp
  • Mushroom
  • Pineapple
  • Tomatoes
  • Lemon Grass
  • Galangal
  • Kaffir Lime Leaves
  • Tom Yum Paste
  • Fish Sauce
  • Lime
  • Shallots
  • Fresh Thai Chili

------------------------------------------------

Directions

  1. Chop some Mushrooms, Shallots, Tomatoes, Fresh Thai Chili, and pineapple and set to side.
  2. Fill your cooking pot with water and bring to boil.
  3. Bruise your Lemon Grass and add into pot.
  4. Add in the rest of your aroma's (Kaffir Lime Leaves and Galangal).
  5. Add Tom Yum Paste until desired taste is achieved.
  6. Add Fish sauce until desired taste is achieved.
  7. Add some Fresh Thai Chili until desired heat is achieved.
  8. Add your proteins (Shrimp, Mushrooms, Pineapple, Tomatoes).
  9. Once fully cooked, turn off stove then squeeze some Lime Juice until desire taste is achieved.
  10. Serve and enjoy.
